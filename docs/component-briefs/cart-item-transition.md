# Brief — Cart Item Transition

- **Problem:** a real cart line isn't a static product card — it arrives, its quantity/total change (and can fail to save), a variant is swapped, the price moves, stock turns limited/unavailable, it's removed, and it's undone. Apps hand-roll these transitions inconsistently and drop focus/announcements. This is one **app-controlled cart line** that animates those states honestly. It is NOT a cart, checkout, or store, and never mutates a backend or payment.
- **Use case:** cart/mini-cart line, saved-for-later row, order-review line, subscription line — anywhere a single mutable line needs optimistic edits with rollback.
- **Item data (app-owned):** `id, productName, variantSummary?, image?, unitPrice, quantity, total, previousPrice?, discount?{label,amount?}, availability?, inventoryMessage?, fulfilmentMessage?, subscriptionInterval?, metadata?`.
- **Main states:** adding · active · updating · removing · removed · unavailable · quantity-limited · price-changed · failed · restoring (derived, reported via `onStateChange`).
- **Main interaction:** step quantity via labelled ± buttons → optimistic quantity + total shown immediately → app's async `onQuantityChange` commits or **rolls back** with a failed banner + Retry; remove (optional inline confirm) → collapses to an Undo bar → `onUndoRemove` restores. Variant/price/availability are pushed by the app and animate on change.
- **Animation purpose:** brief, interruptible arrival, quantity/total morph (`useAnimatedNumber`), variant-summary crossfade, price-change treatment, removal collapse, undo restore. No fly-to-cart arc, no manufactured urgency.
- **API sketch:** `item`, `minQuantity?`, `maxQuantity?`, `onQuantityChange?`, `onRemove?`, `onUndoRemove?`, `onChangeVariant?`, `onSaveForLater?`, `onRetry?`, `confirmRemove?`, `formatPrice?/currency/locale`, `mutationState?`, `onStateChange?`, `enterOnMount?`, `layout?`.
- **Accessibility:** quantity steppers are real labelled `<button>`s with a text readout; price and price-change stated as **text** (old→new, arrow glyph — never colour/animation alone); limited/unavailable stated in words + icon; remove/undo keyboard operable; **focus preserved** on the pressed quantity control after a change, **moves to Undo** after removal, and **falls back** to Remove (then root) after undo; polite live announcements; every transition renders final under reduced motion.
- **Mobile behavior:** `layout="compact"`; stepper + actions wrap; all targets ≥32–44px; thumbnail scales down.
- **Dependencies:** motion + `@motionkit/utils` (cn) + `@motionkit/primitives` (`useOptimisticAction`, `useReducedMotion`, `useAnimatedNumber`, `formatNumber`, `statusVars`). No new deps; no `next/*`, Node built-ins, or Remotion.
- **Similarity concern:** avoid a generic quantity-stepper/product-card clone; differentiated by the optimistic-rollback + failed/retry flow, honest price-change/availability treatment, and undo focus management. Low.
- **Tier:** Free.
- **Release criteria:** rapid 12-point gate + optimistic rollback on failed mutation + focus preserved after quantity change + focus fallback after removal/undo + price/availability as text verified.
